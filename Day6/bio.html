Savitribai Phule

Savitribai Phule was born on 3 January 1831 at the village of Naigaon in Satara District, Maharashtra. Her birthplace is about fifteen km (9.3 mi) from Shirval and about 50 km (31 mi) from Pune.[7] Savitribai Phule was the youngest daughter of Lakshmi and Khandoji Nevase Patil, both of whom belonged to the Mali Community.
